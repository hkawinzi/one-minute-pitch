HTML Forms

CC,YY,MM,DD,mod are short forms of

  1. CC - is the century digits. For example 1989 has CC = 19
  2. YY - is the Year digits (1989 has YY = 89)
  3. MM - is the Month
  4. DD - is the Day of the month
  5. mod - is the modulus function ( % )--A modulus function is a function which gives a positive output irrespective of the input
